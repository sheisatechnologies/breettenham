The Location

Brettenham House’s location embodies the spirit of the building, a seamless blend of fascinating heritage with the very best in modern culture and offering a space where great things can be achieved.

Local Area

With illustrious neighbours Somerset House and the world famous Savoy Hotel to either side of the building, the vibrant Covent Garden to the north and the South Bank immediately opposite to the south, there really is everything that you could want on your doorstep, and plenty of hidden gems to uncover.
